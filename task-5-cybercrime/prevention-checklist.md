# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

1. **UPI PIN Rule:** Remember, a UPI PIN is only required to *send* money, never to *receive* it.
2. **Verify Identity:** Never trust "buyers" or "recruiters" on social media without verifying their identity through a phone call or official email.
3. **Strong Passwords:** Use a mix of symbols, numbers, and letters for your university and bank accounts.
4. **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA on your WhatsApp, Instagram, and Gmail to prevent identity theft.
5. **Avoid Public Wi-Fi:** Do not perform bank transactions or login to sensitive accounts using free public Wi-Fi at cafes or stations.
6. **Think Before You Click:** Avoid clicking on "Free Internship" or "Job Offer" links sent via SMS or unknown WhatsApp numbers.
7. **Social Media Privacy:** Do not post your phone number or home address publicly on social media profiles.
8. **Official Apps Only:** Download banking and payment apps only from the official Play Store or App Store.

### How to Report Cybercrime in India:
* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **National Helpline Number:** **1930** (Call immediately if you lose money).
