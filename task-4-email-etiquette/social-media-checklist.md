# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

| Do's (Responsible Use) | Don'ts (Avoid These) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1. Privacy Settings:** Update your privacy settings regularly. | **1. Don't Post Sensitive Info:** Never share OTP, Address, or ID. |
| **2. Professionalism:** Keep your profile professional for recruiters. | **2. Don't Cyberbully:** Avoid hateful comments or trolling. |
| **3. Verify News:** Fact-check any news before sharing it. | **3. Don't Post During Class:** Stay away from social media in lectures. |
| **4. Secure Accounts:** Use Strong Passwords and 2FA. | **4. Don't Click Unknown Links:** Avoid phishing and scam links. |
| **5. Respect Others:** Be polite in online discussions. | **5. Don't Overshare:** Avoid posting live locations for safety. |
